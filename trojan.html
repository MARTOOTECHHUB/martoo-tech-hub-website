What is Trojan?

Trojan is a protocol designed to provide a secure and reliable connection for users while maintaining high performance. It disguises its traffic as regular HTTPS traffic, making it difficult to detect.

Key Features of Trojan

  • Obfuscation to avoid detection
  • Efficient performance with minimal latency
  • Easy to set up and configure
